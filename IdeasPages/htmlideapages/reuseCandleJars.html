Reuse Candle Jars

Materials

  1. A single candle jar
  2. A Knife or Spoon

Candle jars can be used to store various items.
    First, grab a candle jar and take out the wax left inside with a spoon or knife. Then we can wash the jar thoroughly with soap. From there we can put anything inside! We can also close the lid if the jar has one. The candle jar can be used to store candies, fruits, coins, pens and other small items.
