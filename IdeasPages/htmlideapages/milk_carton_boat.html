Milk Carton Pirate Ship

Materials

  1. An empty, rinsed out Milk Carton.
  2. An empty, rinsed out K-Cup.
  3. A chopstick (or candy stick, sturdy straw, or twig).
  4. Paper.
  5. Glue.
  6. String.
  7. Optional – markers or paint to decorate the ship. You can also use fabric or laminated paper to help sails stand up to some splashing!
  8. Scissors

Instructions:
Turn your carton on its side, creating a point at the front of what will be your ship. Cut the sides in a small slope, or into whatever shape you’d like your boat to have. Paint carton if you wish.
Design and cut sails. Keep in mind the width of your boat carton.
Glue your K-Cup down in the center of your milk carton.
Cut a hole at the top and bottom of the sails and weave your chopstick or straw through the holes, to secure it.
Decorate your boat! We went for a Pirate theme!
