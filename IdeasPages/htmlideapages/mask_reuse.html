Reusing Face Masks

Materials

  1. Face Mask
  2. Scissors

Instructions:
Cut open the top of the mask and remove the metal band to use it as a twist-tie
This twist-tie should be stronger and last longer than typical metal twist-ties
You can also remove the elastics and reuse them as hair-ties
